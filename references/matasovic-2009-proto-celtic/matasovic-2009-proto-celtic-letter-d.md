### *damo/a- 'retinue' [Noun]
GOID: OIr. dam [a f]

 BRET: MBret. deufJ, MoBret. deunv [m] 'son-in-law'
 co: OCo. dofgl. gener
 PIE: *domo- 'household, home' (IEW: 198f.)
 COGN: Lat. domus 'home', Gr. domos, OCS domb
 ETYM: The OW hapax daum has an alternative reading dauu (Falileyev,

but with little justification. In PIE, the original root-noun *dom may have
been connected with the root *demhr 'build' (Gr. demo, Dor. neodmatos
'newly built'), if the development was *domhzs > *dom (as assumed by
LIV).
