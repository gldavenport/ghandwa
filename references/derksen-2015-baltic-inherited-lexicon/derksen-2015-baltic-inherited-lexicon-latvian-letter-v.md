### vikt
**LATV** vikt 'bend' [i2], isg. pres. vicu, vikstu, isg. pret. viku, vicu
The only accented form in ME and EH is vikt2 (Biel.) 'sich biegen'. If De Vaan is
correct about the original meaning of the root that we find in →veikti, the meaning
of this Latvian verb is archaic.
