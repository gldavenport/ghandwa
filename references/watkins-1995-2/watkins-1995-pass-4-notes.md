# Watkins 1995 — Fourth-Pass Notes

Pass 4 focused on two tasks requested after pass 3:

1. Greek/non-Latin form audit.
2. Index of Words reconstruction.

## Method

- Started from `watkins-1995-clean-pass-3.md`.
- Rendered/used page images for targeted pages with unresolved Greek/non-Latin placeholders.
- Used OCR over rendered page images for the Index of Words, printed pp. 601-613 / PDF pages 618-630.
- Replaced only forms that were either confident or explicitly marked as still uncertain.

## Results

- The exact generic marker `[unclear: non-Latin form omitted in OCR]` has been removed from the main Markdown.
- Remaining unresolved readings are now specific rather than generic.
- The Index of Words has been rebuilt as a page-anchored OCR reconstruction.
- Stress-test results are documented in `watkins-1995-stress-tests-pass-4.md`.

## Remaining limitations

- This is not a full character-authoritative edition.
- Greek accents/breathings, metrical symbols, and some index headwords remain likely to need manual proofing.
- The bibliography has not yet received a dedicated normalization pass.
- Formula/form extraction has not yet been done as a separate dataset.
