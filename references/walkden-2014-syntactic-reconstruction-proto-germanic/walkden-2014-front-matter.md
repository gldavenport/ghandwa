Theresa Biberauer deserves a special mention for her seemingly infinite energy and unfailing enthusiasm, and for continually pointing me in useful directions. Ian Roberts has been an amiable, accessible guru, who has been extremely supportive during my years as a graduate student and beyond, quite apart from being a fantastic source of information on virtually every conceivable linguistic topic.
