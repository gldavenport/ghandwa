## Ghandwa relevance

The primary Koch monograph on Celto-Germanic shared vocabulary. Directly complements van Sluis et al. 2023 and Koch 2024. High priority for LEX work: provides etymological and comparative data on post-PIE vocabulary shared by Celtic and Germanic. Together with van Sluis, this is the most comprehensive treatment of the Celto-Germanic lexical question available.
