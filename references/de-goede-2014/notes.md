# Notes — de Goede 2014

## Bibliographic details

**Tim de Goede. *Derivational Morphology: New Perspectives on the Italo-Celtic Hypothesis*.**  
BA/MA thesis (Linguistics: Structure and Variation in the Languages of the World), Leiden University, 2014.  
First reader: Dr. M. A. C. de Vaan. Second reader: Prof. dr. A. Lubotsky.

## Content summary

Examines shared derivational suffixes (including suffixes with glides and liquids) as morphological evidence for or against the Italo-Celtic hypothesis. Supervised by de Vaan, so methodologically aligned with EDL.
