According to Pokorny (IEW: 224), OCS dažda is analogical after *Ydja (the
substantive jažda is not attested in OCS). The jā-stem is based on the reduplicated
root *dad- < *dodh₃- (' *dàti), which shows the effects of Winter’s law.
