### maldenikis
**OPR** maldenikis 'child'
**BSL** *moldenikos
**PSL** *moldenbcb m. jo 'infant, child, youth'
**SL** OCS mladenbcb 'infant'; mladenbcb 'infant'; Ru. mladenec 'infant'; Cz.
          mladenec (arch., dial.) 'child, youth, adolescent'; Sllc. mladenec 'youth,
          unmarried young man'; Pl. mlodzieniec 'youth'; SCr. mliidjenac 'infant, child,
          youth'; Sln. mladęnx 'youth'
A derivative of the word for 'young' (-→ OPr. maldai).
