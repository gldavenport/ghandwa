root *soi-, which could have given to rise to new apophonic grades *sei- and *si-. The
root of *sYt! seems to be acute, however. Perhaps the zero grade *sh'i-provided the
basis for a new full grade *soh'i (after all, *Ce/oHI : *CHI is a common type of

semantic connection is obvious.
