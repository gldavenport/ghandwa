# Notes — Kroonen 2016

## Bibliographic details

**Guus Kroonen. "On the Origin of Greek μῆλον, Latin málum, Albanian mollë and Hittite sam(a)lu- 'apple'."**  
Year: 2016. Journal/volume: TBD — verify from PDF metadata or publication page.  
Institution at time of writing: Institute for Nordic Studies and Linguistics, University of Copenhagen.

## Content summary

Argues that the commonly reconstructed *meh₂lo- for 'apple' should be corrected to *smh₂l-, based on the addition of Hittite sam(a)lu- as a comparandum. Methodology: regular sound change correspondences across branches; typologically motivated proto-form revision.
