# Notes — Kroonen 2012

## Bibliographic details

**Guus Kroonen. "Non-Indo-European root nouns in Germanic: evidence in support of the Agricultural Substrate Hypothesis."**  
Year: 2012. Journal/volume: TBD — verify from PDF metadata or publication page.  
Institution at time of writing: Department of Scandinavian Studies and Linguistics, Copenhagen University.

## Content summary

Argues that a significant portion of Germanic vocabulary lacking Indo-European etymology reflects a pre-IE agricultural substrate. Examines root nouns in Germanic as a morphological class that may preserve substrate material. Connects to the broader Agricultural Substrate Hypothesis for Germanic.
