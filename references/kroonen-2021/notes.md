## Ghandwa relevance

Directly relevant: *sneigʷʰ- is a labiovelar root and Ghandwa preserves labiovelars. The Ghandwa reflex would be *sneɣʷ- or similar depending on environment. Check against the transformer and lexicon for any snow/falling vocabulary. Multi-author with Wigman and Thorsø — same research cluster as Kroonen 2022 and Wigman 2023.
