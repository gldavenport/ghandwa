4.1 Word list The wordlist is arranged alphabetically, grouping all variants of the same Latin letter (velar, labiovelar, palatal, aspirated, etc.) into one section for convenience’s sake. Exempt from this treatment are the laryngeals that, true to their cover sign, is listed under H, and then numerically.
