Index
The index is arranged alphabetically. Variations in spelling of the initial letter gets two (or
three) separate entries. The order of IE branches follows the stratification (§ 1.3), thus:
Anatolian, Tocharian, Italic, Celtic, Germanic, Baltic, Slavic, Albanian, Greek, fragmentary
languages, Armenian, Indo-Iranian
Then follow non-IE languages in the following order:
NW Caucasian, NE Caucasian, North Caucasian (reconstructions only), Hurro-Urartian,
Kartvelian, Uralic (incl. Yukaghir), Semitic, Afro-Asiatic, Sumerian, Turkic, other languages
Separate treatment is then granted reconstructions for PIE and glosses for PIE
