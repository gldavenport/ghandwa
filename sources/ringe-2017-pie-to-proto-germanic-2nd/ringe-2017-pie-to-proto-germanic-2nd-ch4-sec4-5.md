328     Proto-Germanic


4.5 PGmc syntax
The study of PGmc syntax is still in its infancy. In the current state of the field it is
best approached in terms of developments away from PIE syntax (which has actually
received more attention), as exemplified in Section 3.5, and in terms of the syntax of
the attested languages; see vol. ii, Chapter 8, for a description of the syntax of OE. The
best interim study by far is Walkden 2014, to which the reader is referred for much
further information.
