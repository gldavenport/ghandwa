## Noun Phrase Order

**Default order: noun + adjective** (head-initial for adjectival modification).

Examples:
- *ɣoistós megus* 'great monster' (noun *ɣoistós* + adjective *megus*)

**Numerals, possessive pronouns, determiners:** These may precede the noun. The precise rule governing pre-nominal elements is not yet settled.

**Compounds:** Within a compound, a qualifying first member precedes the head noun (§4.4.1). This is lexical structure, not syntax, and does not override the noun-adjective order of ordinary NPs.

## Titles and Apposition

Appositional or title-like forms follow the noun:

- *ɣoistós Māks* 'the monster, the Great One' — *Māks* as appositional title

*Māks* is restricted to nominative and vocative singular in this function. See lexicon for entry details.

## Further Syntax

Remaining syntax (clause order, subordination, case government, valency, etc.) is not yet specified. See §2.5 for the PIE baseline.
