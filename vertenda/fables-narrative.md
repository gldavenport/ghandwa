## Candidates Under Consideration

**Grimm:**
- *Der Wolf und die sieben jungen Geißlein* ("The Wolf and the Seven Young Goats") — short, formulaic, heavy on dialogue and repetition. Tests sequential narrative and number vocabulary.
- *Das Tapfere Schneiderlein* ("The Brave Little Tailor") — longer; has martial register elements.

**Aesop / Latin fable tradition:**
- *The Tortoise and the Hare* — short, punchy, moral register. Phaedrus's Latin version is clean and very short.
- *The Fox and the Crow* — also from Phaedrus; tests flattery/speech vocabulary.

**Custom Ghandwa original:**
A short prose narrative composed in Ghandwa rather than translated — tests the same skills while also functioning as a `corpus/` documentary text. This may ultimately be more valuable than a translation for this slot.
